How to Play the Overthinker Monster Game

Welcome to the Overthinker Monster Game! Follow these simple steps to play:

1. Enter Keywords:

- Start by entering two keywords of your choice in the input fields provided.

2. Start the Game:

- Click on the "Start Game" button to begin the challenge.

3. Make Your Choice:

- Once the game starts, you will see your two keywords displayed as clickable buttons.

- You have 10 seconds to choose one of the keywords by clicking on it.

4. Win or Lose:

- If you successfully click on one of the keywords within 10 seconds, you win the game!

- If you do not click on any keyword within 10 seconds, the monster wins the game.

Good luck and have fun!
